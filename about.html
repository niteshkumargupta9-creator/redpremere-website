Our Story

A lean, registered brand solutions company helping local businesses look professional, stay connected, and grow consistently.

Who We Are

One Partner.
Everything You Need.

Red Premere Enterprises started with a simple observation — local businesses in Varanasi and across UP often spend too much chasing multiple vendors for their branding needs. One vendor for a website, another for t-shirts, another for bottles, another for visiting cards.

We built Red Premere to fix that. Under one GST registration, one invoice, and one point of contact — you get a complete brand: a website that your QR codes link to, bottles your customers drink from, merch your staff wears, and cards people tap to reach you.

"We're not just a printing company. We're the brand infrastructure for local businesses who want to look like they mean business."

Our consumer brand Honestbro (honestbro.online) handles streetwear and direct-to-consumer merchandise, while Red Premere Enterprises handles all B2B and corporate client work.
